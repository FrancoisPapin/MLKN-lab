🔹 Hierarchy Overview

The MLKN-lab hierarchy organizes scientific knowledge into **5 layers**:

  1. Core Domains: 6 broad categories (e.g., Social Sciences & Humanities, Natural Sciences).
  2. Academic Disciplines: 25 fields (e.g., Psychology, Computer Science).
  3. Subdisciplines: 235 specialized areas (e.g., Cognitive Psychology, AI Ethics).
  4. Core Thematic Domains: Thematic groupings within subdisciplines.
  5. Main Concepts: Specific topics or ideas (e.g., "Attention", "Neural Networks").
