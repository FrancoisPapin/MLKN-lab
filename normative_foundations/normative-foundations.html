The Normative Foundations of MLKN.lab

Principles, Values, and Societal Dreams for a Connected Knowledge Ecosystem.

Knowledge is not just about what we know—it’s about how we should act. The Normative Foundations of MLKN.lab is a meta-layer that maps values, principles, ethics, laws, and norms to our polyhierarchical knowledge network.

This page explores the philosophical, ethical, and societal dimensions of MLKN.lab, contextualizing our research focus within a broader movement to build a more connected, ethical, and knowledgeable society.

  • Great publications that define our normative framework.

  • Societal dreams that inspire our work.

Principles and Values of MLKN.lab

MLKN.lab is built on a foundation of principles and values that guide our research, design, and vision for the future of knowledge.

⬢

Polyhierarchy

Knowledge is not linear or flat—it’s multidimensional and interconnected. MLKN.lab’s polyhierarchical model allows us to represent knowledge as a dynamic, layered network.

Connected to:
  • Research Focus: Computational epistemology, network science.
  • Normative Layer: Systems thinking, complexity.
⬢

Open Science

Knowledge should be accessible, transparent, and reproducible. MLKN.lab is committed to open access.

Connected to:
  • Research Focus: Open Access Monographs, Scientific References.
  • Normative Layer: Transparency, collaboration.
⬢

Interdisciplinarity

The most pressing challenges require interdisciplinary solutions. MLKN.lab breaks down silos between AI, education, human rights.

Connected to:
  • Research Focus: Multilayer networks, hypergraphs.
  • Normative Layer: Collaboration, diversity.
⬢

Ethical Knowledge

Knowledge has ethical implications. MLKN.lab integrates ethical frameworks into our knowledge network.

Connected to:
  • Research Focus: Human-AI Interaction, Cognitive Architectures.
  • Normative Layer: Ethics, human rights.
⬢

Societal Impact

Knowledge should serve society. MLKN.lab is designed to have a real-world impact.

Connected to:
  • Research Focus: Knowledge Infrastructure, Applications.
  • Normative Layer: Justice, equity, sustainability.
