Curated List of Key References

MLKN.lab’s Open Access Monographs collection is a curated library of interdisciplinary, open-access books that underpin the MLKN.model (computational epistemology) and MLKN.hypergraph (polyhierarchical knowledge network). These monographs span meta-science, cognitive architectures, network theory, systems science, and AI, providing the theoretical depth and methodological rigor to explore the structural topology of scientific knowledge.
